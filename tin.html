Where is Şile in Istanbul?
Şile is a district of Istanbul located in the Asian part of the metropolis, about 70 kilometers from the city center. It has a population of about 29,000 inhabitants, although during the hottest months, this number increases since it is a very popular area for summer houses. It is a district also known for being on the shores of the Black Sea, for its nature and delicate charm.
